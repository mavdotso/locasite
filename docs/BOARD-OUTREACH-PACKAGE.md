## Section 4 — Send Instructions (Gmail + YAMM)

YAMM (Yet Another Mail Merge) is a free Gmail add-on that sends personalized emails to a list from Google Sheets. Recommended for 17+ sends — faster and fewer errors than manual one-by-one.

### Option A: YAMM Mail Merge (Recommended)

**One-time setup (5 minutes):**

1. Install YAMM free add-on from Google Workspace Marketplace: search "Yet Another Mail Merge"
2. Open your tracking Google Sheet
3. Make sure columns are named exactly: `email`, `first_name`, `business_name`, `city`, `claim_link`

**For each send day:**

1. **Create a Gmail draft** (do not send it — just save as draft). Use these variable placeholders:
   - `{{first_name}}` where you want the person's first name
   - `{{business_name}}` where you want the business name
   - `{{city}}` where you want the city/neighborhood
   - `{{claim_link}}` where you want the claim link
   - Subject line also accepts variables: `{{business_name}}, your website is ready`

2. **Open your Google Sheet** → Extensions → Yet Another Mail Merge → Start Mail Merge

3. **Select your Gmail draft** from the dropdown

4. **Map columns**: YAMM will ask which sheet column maps to which variable. Match:
   - Sheet column `email` → Recipient email
   - Sheet column `first_name` → `{{first_name}}`
   - Sheet column `business_name` → `{{business_name}}`
   - etc.

5. **Send a test email** to your own address first — confirm all variables resolve (no `{{` brackets remaining)

6. **Filter your sheet** before each send to only include leads for that send day (not already replied or claimed)

7. **Send to full filtered list**

8. **Log the send timestamp** in your tracking sheet (`email_1_sent`, `email_2_sent`, etc.)

## Section 5 — Follow-Up Schedule

### The 3-Email Sequence (per lead, over 8 days)

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **Day 1** | Send Email 1 to all 17 leads (Variant A or B based on category) |
| **Day 4** | Send Email 2 to all leads who have NOT replied and NOT claimed |
| **Day 8** | Send Email 3 to all leads who have NOT replied and NOT claimed |
| **Day 9+** | Stop. No further contact. Monitor replies and conversions. |

### Suppression — Remove Before Each Send

Before Day 4 and Day 8 sends, remove from list:
- Anyone who replied (any reply — positive, negative, or confused)
- Anyone who clicked the claim link and completed it
- Anyone who said "remove me," "unsubscribe," or equivalent

Mark suppressed leads in the tracking sheet with status `opt_out` or `replied-not-interested`.

### Stop Rules — Per Lead

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Lead replies (any reply) | Stop sequence immediately for this lead |
| Lead claims their site | Stop sequence immediately for this lead |
| Lead says "remove me" | Stop and mark `opt_out` — never contact again |
| Email 3 sent | Sequence complete — no 4th email ever |

## Section 7 — Tracking Sheet Setup

Create a Google Sheet with these columns (one row per business):

```
business_name | category | email | claim_link | variant | email_1_sent | email_2_sent | email_3_sent | replied_at | reply_type | claimed_at | paid_at | status | notes
```

**Category values:** `restaurant` or `home-services`

**Variant values:** `A-restaurant` or `B-home-services`

**Status lifecycle:**
```
queued → e1_sent → e2_sent → e3_sent → replied / claimed / paid / opt_out / no-response
```

**Status definitions:**

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `queued` | On list, not yet emailed |
| `e1_sent` | Email 1 sent |
| `e2_sent` | Email 2 sent |
| `e3_sent` | Email 3 sent — sequence complete |
| `replied-interested` | Replied positively or neutrally |
| `replied-not-interested` | Declined |
| `claimed` | Completed claim flow, payment pending |
| `paid` | Converted — paying customer ($149 + $9/mo) |
| `opt_out` | Requested removal — do not contact |
| `bounced` | Email bounced |
| `no-response` | Full sequence complete, no reply |
