# Locasite — Mission & Vision

## What is Locasite?
Business Site Generator SaaS that creates professional websites for local businesses by scraping Google Maps data. Subdomain-based multi-tenancy (e.g., `joes-pizza.locasite.com`).

## Target Users
- Local businesses without websites
- Business owners who want a quick online presence
- Service providers who need a simple landing page

## Value Proposition
1. **Zero effort**: Paste Google Maps URL → Get a website
2. **Instant setup**: No coding, no design skills needed
3. **Professional look**: Templates optimized for local businesses
4. **Editable**: Business owners can claim and customize

## Monetization
- Free tier: Basic site with Locasite branding
- Paid tier: Custom domain, remove branding, analytics
- Enterprise: White-label for agencies

## Tech Stack
- Next.js 15 + React 19
- Convex (database, auth, real-time)
- Tinybird (analytics)
- OpenAI SDK (AI chat features)
- Stripe (payments)
